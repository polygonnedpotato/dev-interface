The Web Bluetooth, WebUSB, Web Serial, Web NFC, and WebHID APIs directly connects your browser to your physical devices that are directly or indirectly connected to your computer. This can include webcams, security keys, and even game controllers. While this can be helpful in some cases, the misusage of said APIs can cause temporary or even permanent damage to those devices.
Unless you know exactly what you're doing, please use this app ONLY for testing OR for retriving device details.
